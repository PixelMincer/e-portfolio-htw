My Study

So why do I study DBM at HTW Chur? First and foremost I find it a very important skill in todays society to be able to innovate and think outside the box. With this said I think the DBM study gives me the following benefits:

  • Create and test new solutions directly with the market
  • Getting to know project management
  • Staying on the rising edge of technology
  • Getting to know basic concepts in order to run a business

I think these skills and experiences are really. In my free time and during my job I have more of the technical aspects and during study I learn really valuable skills. This way I can get the best of both worlds.

My Vision

For me a vision has to do with what I love the most. This may not bring in the most amount of money or be the most easiest thing but I will definiely be the thing I will enjoy the most. With this said I would like to try following my dream which is becoming a video game developer. Here I have multiple possiblities:

  • Create a startup
  • Join an existing company

Lets analyze these opportunities. If I were to create a startup I would need the necessary amount of founding money. Besides money I would need the necesary people. As you can imagine making a video games involves various disciplines such as programming, creating assets, composing music or composing the scenario just to name a few. Then there is the problem with reaching the critical mass once the product is finished which is not perticularly easy esspecially in Switzerland.

Joining an existing company is more feasable but also quite a task in itself. For one thing game developing companies in Switzerland are very rare. Your best shot is therefore going abroad to a country like Japan or USA. Besides the difficulty of landing a job there is also a considerable language barrier to keep in mind.

So I must be nuts to try and archieve something like this right? Well otherwise it would be a pretty boring vision there has to be some sort of challenge to it. For me it means I have to develop and hone the necessary skills to make this possible one step at a time. A wall is also not build in a day but brick by brick, day by day, step by step.
